    • About myself

    My name is Nasim Reza, born and raised in Bangladesh. I started my studies in my homeland but I knew I had to move to have better opportunities. At the age of 19, I moved to Berlin and started studying International Business but for personal reasons, I had to stop and start working. I found my way in the kitchen and ended up as a chef. As much as I love cooking, I will always regret terminating my studies. Nevertheless, after 9 years living in Berlin, I decided to follow my childhood dream, so I started studying web development and since then I never looked back!

    When I’m not traveling and resting, I spend my days experimenting with coding while searching further information to become a better web developer. As they say, you never know enough! I’m curious, I search perfection and most of all I’m patient so I always end up with great results. I build websites that give life to any primitive ideas!
